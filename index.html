This Privacy Policy explains how Bhram Interactive ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you play Shantipalace: The Last Stop on Android or iOS. By playing our game, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information automatically when you use the app:

Data Type Examples Why We Collect It
Device Information Device model, OS version, screen size Game compatibility and performance
Advertising Identifier GAID (Android), IDFA (iOS) Showing relevant ads via LevelPlay / ironSource
App Usage Data Chapters played, session length, in-game events Analytics to improve the game
Approximate Location Country / region (not GPS) Ad targeting and language settings
App Version Version number of the installed app Bug tracking and update management

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To run and improve the game experience
  • To display advertisements (interstitial and rewarded video ads)
  • To analyze gameplay patterns and fix bugs
  • To measure how players engage with each chapter and story moment
  • To ensure technical compatibility across different devices

3. Advertising IMPORTANT

Shantipalace: The Last Stop is free to play and supported by advertisements. We use the following ad services:

Unity LevelPlay (ironSource)
LevelPlay is our primary ad mediation platform. It may collect your device's advertising identifier and other technical data to serve relevant ads. LevelPlay may work with multiple ad networks on our behalf.

Privacy Policy: https://www.is.com/privacy-policy/
Unity Analytics
We use Unity Analytics to track in-game events such as chapter completions, session length, and ad interactions. This data is anonymised and used only to improve the game.

Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Ads shown in the game include:

  • Interstitial ads — shown between chapters
  • Rewarded video ads — optional, watched in exchange for in-game hints

You are never forced to watch a rewarded ad. They are always optional.

4. iOS — App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14 and above, we will ask for your permission before tracking your activity across apps and websites using your device's IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers).

  • If you allow tracking → you may see more relevant ads
  • If you deny tracking → you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant
  • The game works fully either way

You can change your tracking preference at any time in: iPhone Settings → Privacy → Tracking

5. In-App Purchases

Shantipalace: The Last Stop offers optional in-app purchases such as unlocking the full game or removing ads. All purchases are processed securely through:

  • Google Play Billing (Android)
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (iOS)

We do not store or process your payment information directly. All billing is handled by Google and Apple under their respective privacy policies.

6. Do We Share Your Data?

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted third-party service providers strictly to operate the game:

Third Party Purpose

ironSource (is.com) Ad serving and mediation

All third parties are required to handle your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws including GDPR and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023.

7. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months and then automatically deleted or anonymised. Advertising identifiers follow the retention policies of the respective ad networks.

You can reset your advertising identifier at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Reset Advertising ID (or turn off tracking)
